### Purpose of the PAC

The Planning Advisory Commitee (PAC) is key to creating a comprehensive plan that addresses community concerns and visions. It composed of interested residents, community organization and group leaders, business and property owners, and other stakeholders. The PAC help guide the planning process, review draft materials and recommendations, and promote the project to the public.
