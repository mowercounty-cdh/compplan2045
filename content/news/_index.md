**Follow this page for any relevant news and updates related to the City and County’s Comprehensive Plans.**

#### NEW! _Mower County and City of Austin adopted their Comprehensive Plans!_

The City of Austin City Council officially adopted the City of Austin Comprehensive Plan on June 1, 2026. The Mower County Planning Commission officially adopted the Mower County Comprehensive Plan on May 12, 2026. [View the Plans](/draftplans/)

#### _The Project Team Releases a DRAFT of the “Playbook”_

Additional to the traditional comprehensive planning, the Comprehensive Plans include a “Playbook” with concepts and strategies for dynamic food systems, downtown development frameworks, tourism and entertainment, arts and cultural elements, a reframed economic analysis, and open space and parks plan. The Playbook provides additional tools for ongoing community engagement and strategies to address emerging issues in new and innovative ways. The Playbook was developed based on best planning practices, market research, and extensive community engagement and involvement. [CLICK HERE TO VIEW THE DRAFT PLAYBOOK.](/documents/DRAFT_2045_Comprehensive_Plan_PLAYBOOK_July_8_2025_COMPRESSED.pdf)

This was part of the County and City’s efforts to engage a broad range of community members as part of the new Comprehensive Plans.
