# VIEW THE PLANS!

#### We are especially grateful to the community members and city staff who shared their experiences and guidance to develop these plans.

**Scroll down to view the plans.**

Since Spring 2024, both Mower County and the City of Austin have been collaborating to develop these plans jointly.

The Comprehensive Plans are roadmaps that guide growth and quality of life for the next 20 years. The Plans set goals and strategies that reflect local priorities for a range of key topics, such as housing, transportation, utilities, community facilities, agricultural, natural and cultural resources, economic development, intergovernmental cooperation, and land use. They are intended to be used and referenced daily as part of the county and city’s business by residents, city staff, developers, and other stakeholders.

#### NOTES: The Mower County Planning Commission officially adopted the Mower County Comprehensive Plan on May 12, 2026. The City of Austin City Council officially adopted the City of Austin Comprehensive Plan on June 1, 2026.

### **View the “PLAYBOOK”**

**Additional to traditional comprehensive planning, the Comprehensive Plans include a “Playbook” with concepts and strategies for dynamic food systems, downtown development frameworks, tourism and entertainment, arts and cultural elements, a reframed economic analysis, and open space and parks plan.** The Playbook provides additional tools for ongoing community engagement and strategies to address emerging issues in new and innovative ways. The Playbook was developed based on best planning practices, market research, and extensive community engagement and involvement.
